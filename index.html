You are collecting hummingbirds for your aviary. You will be given a number goal for the amount of hummingbirds you need to collect.

Each hummingbird picture contains a hidden number. Click on each one to add that number to your total count.

You win the game by matching your total count exactly to the Goal. If your count goes over the Goal, you lose!

Each time when the game re-starts, the hidden number for each hummingbird will change.
