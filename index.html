You are an enthusiastic bird-watcher and photographer. You will be given a Goal for the total number of pictures you need to capture of these beautiful hummingbirds.

There are four crystal hummingbirds. By clicking on each one you will snap a certain number of pictures.

You win the game by matching your total score exactly to your Goal. You lose the game if your total score goes above the Goal.

The value of each hummingbird is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game re-starts, the game will change the values of each hummingbird.
